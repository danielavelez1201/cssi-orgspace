Welcome to orgspace

The space for your organization.




Mission Vision
To provide a space for organizations that seek to make a positive impact on communities. To serve as the preferred platform for anyone seeking to start or join a good cause.


Find Others Like You


At orgspace you will find a host of people committed to making a positive impact on society. Get inspired on our search page.


Locate, Create and Attend Community Events


Host events and let the community feel your presence. You can help support local organizers and attend events close to your current location.


Collaborate With Other Organizers


If you're an established organizer, you can pool resources with other organizers and maximize your reach.


Donate to Good Causes


Give a helping hand and support organizers having events in your community. You never know whose life you will impact.
